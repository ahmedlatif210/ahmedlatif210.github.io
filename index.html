Privacy Policy — Elehanssy ERP

Last updated: 2026-03-15

This Privacy Policy describes how Elehanssy ERP (the "App"), developed by Ahmed Latif ("we", "us"), collects, uses, stores, and shares data when users enable Google Drive backup features.

1. Information We Access

  • When you authorize Google Drive backup, the App requests access to your Google Drive scope: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file.
  • This allows the App to create and manage files it creates in your Drive (backup files). The App does not request broad Drive or Gmail read access.

2. Purpose of Access

  • Create timestamped database backups in your Google Drive when you explicitly request a backup.
  • Allow users to restore the application database from backups stored in their Drive.

3. Data Retention and Storage

  • Backup files are stored directly in your Google Drive; we do not upload these backup files to any third-party servers.
  • Locally, the App stores: an OAuth token (access + refresh token) in the application user data folder to enable automatic token refresh and uploads.
  • Tokens are stored as a JSON file named google_drive_tokens.json under the application user data directory.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We will not share your backup files or account tokens with third parties.
  • If you choose to share a backup file from Drive explicitly, it follows Google Drive sharing rules.

5. How to Revoke Access

  • You can revoke the App’s access from your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access.
  • The App also provides a sign-out / revoke option in Settings (if enabled).

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: ahmedlatif.code@gmail.com
